Nancy Liu 刘南希

Hi, I'm Nanxi Liu, a graduating Visual Communication Design student at Shandong University of Art & Design, currently seeking research opportunities in Human-Computer Interaction.

My work sits at the intersection of affective interaction, behavior change, and design for wellbeing — exploring how interactive media can help people perceive, express, and regulate their emotional states without relying on language.

I've built a tangible puzzle system powered by generative AI and emotion recognition APIs to help perfectionists ease into action, and designed a light-responsive environment that adapts in real time to support intergenerational conversation. Along the way, I've also volunteered as a design assistant at an autism care center, developing visual communication systems that give non-verbal youth a way to express themselves. My work has been recognized at the Milan Design Week national exhibition and the International Wandering Poster Biennial, and I received the National Scholarship from China's Ministry of Education in 2025.

Outside of research, I enjoy painting, exploring materials, and experimenting with visual forms and interactive media.
