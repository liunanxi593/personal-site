About Projects

Hi, I'm Nancy Liu, a graduating Visual Communication Design student at Shandong University of Art & Design, currently seeking research opportunities in Human-Computer Interaction.

My research sits at the intersection of affective interaction and art therapy, exploring how non-verbal interactive media can sense emotional states and support behavior change. I have pursued this through two projects: an ambient lighting system using affective computing to facilitate intergenerational communication, and a tangible serious game designed to reduce task avoidance in perfectionist users. Both investigate low-barrier interaction design as a way to lower emotional defensiveness and encourage engagement. Along the way, I've also volunteered as a design assistant at an autism care center, developing visual communication systems that give non-verbal youth a way to express themselves. My work has been recognized at the Milan Design Week national exhibition and the International Wandering Poster Biennial, and I received the National Scholarship from China's Ministry of Education in 2025.

Outside of research, I enjoy painting, exploring materials, and experimenting with visual forms and interactive media.
