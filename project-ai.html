AnxiPuzzle

Background

This project starts from anxiety-related emotions and the difficulty of taking action under emotional pressure. It explores whether affective computing and tangible interaction can turn emotional hesitation into a more approachable form of engagement.

Problem

Many anxiety-related states remain abstract and hard to act on. When emotions are left in the mind as pressure or avoidance, users may find it difficult to begin, even when they know what they need to do.

Approach

This project proposes a serious game in the form of a tangible graphic puzzle. Anxiety-related emotions are translated through affective computing into a visual puzzle experience, allowing users to engage with emotion through physical interaction. After the puzzle is completed, the system also generates a story to help users further process and alleviate the corresponding emotion.
