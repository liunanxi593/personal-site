Intergenerational Communication Design

Background

This project focuses on communication between young and older adults. It begins from the idea that conversation is influenced not only by words, but also by emotional states and the surrounding environment.

Problem

Emotional states in conversation are often difficult to identify in real time, and mismatched emotional atmospheres may limit the depth of communication between different generations.

Approach

This project uses text-based sentiment analysis to identify emotional states in conversation, and dynamically adjusts ambient lighting based on research on color and emotional arousal. In this way, the environment is used as a responsive medium to support deeper communication between young and older adults.
